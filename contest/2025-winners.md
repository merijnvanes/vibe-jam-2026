# Vibe Jam 2025 — Winners

> <https://x.com/levelsio/status/1915127796097290534>

📣 The winners of the 2025 Vibe Code Game Jam sponsored by @boltdotnew + @coderabbitai + @lambdaapi from over 1,000+ games submitted are...

....as judged by @karpathy @timsoret @mrdoob @s13k_ and me

#1 🏆 $10,000
The Great Taxi Assignment by @TomasBencko
Play it 👉 https://great-taxi-assignment.netlify.app
A fun GTA-like taxi driver simulator

The jury said:
"I love it, it's funny, cute, well executed, cool music, great, potential winner"
"Fun game - Single Player GTA style picking up people and dropping them off. You can run them over too. A little bit too easy."
"Crazy taxi, cool vibes, great car physics, feels good, instant fun, level design a bit flat"

#2 🥈 $5,000
Vibeware by @mattgordonxyz
Play it 👉 https://vibeware.vercel.app

The jury said:
"Much fun"

#3 🥉 $2,500
Vector Tango by @scobel
Play it 👉 https://vector-tango.com

The jury said:
"Looks pretty clean/polished, air traffic control simulator"

💖 Thanks to the judges for judging all the games, thanks to the sponsors, thanks to EVERYONE for making all these amazing games!

In the next few days all the games will go live on the site and get connected in the giant Vibeverse with portals!

It was a blast organizing this and after the plane game went viral I think it was a great way to get more distribution for everyone else and get their games played too

Vibe coding still has its limitations but it seems clear that we're all on to something, as the vast majority of people participating never made a game before! That's refreshing and allows new people to enter industries they've never been in before. AI is opening up a lot of creativity and I think that's super exciting! 😊
