# Vibe Jam 2026 — Contest Requirements

## Hard Rules

1. **Web-accessible** — playable in the browser, no downloads
2. **No auth walls** — no login, signup, or account required to play
3. **Free-to-play**
4. **Instant load** — no loading screens, no heavy asset downloads; player must be in-game almost immediately

## Preferences (not required)

- Own domain or subdomain for hosting
- Multiplayer preferred
- Three.js recommended as engine
- May prompt for a username before gameplay

## Deadline

**1 May 2026 at 13:37 UTC**
